About Us

At Paw's & Relax Inn, we understand the importance of peace of mind when leaving your dog behind. We are licensed and Ofqual level 3 qualified. With years of personal experience and a genuine love for animals, we offer a safe and comfortable environment where your dog will receive the attention and care they deserve.

Our Services:

  • Short Stays: Ideal for overnight trips or weekend getaways, our short stays provide a cozy retreat for your dog while you're away. For extended vacations or business trips, we offer long-term boarding options to ensure that your dog is well cared for throughout your absence
  • Long Stays: For extended vacations or business trips, we offer long-term boarding options to ensure that your dog is well cared for throughout your absence.
  • Personalized Care: Every dog is unique, and we tailor our care to meet their individual needs and preferences. We recommend pets come with their own beds, so they can better acclimate to the change of environment away from home. Whether it's regular 30 Minute+ walks, playtime, we ensure that your dog feels right at home.
  • Feeding and Medication: We follow your instructions carefully to maintain your dog's regular feeding schedule and administer any necessary medications or supplements.

Facilities:

  • Comfortable Accommodations: Our facility features cozy sleeping areas and plenty of space for your dog to relax and unwind.
  • Secure Outdoor Space: We have a fenced outdoor area where your dog can enjoy supervised playtime and bathroom breaks.
  • 24/7 Supervision: Your dog's safety and well-being are our top priorities, and our dedicated staff provides round-the-clock supervision.

Reservation Process:

  • Consultation: Contact us to schedule a consultation and discuss your dog's needs and preferences.
  • Reservation: Once you're ready to book, we'll reserve your dog's spot for the desired dates
  • Drop-offs: On the day of drop-off, you can bring your dog to our facility, where we'll provide a warm welcome and ensure a smooth transition.
  • Updates: Throughout your dog's stay, we'll provide regular updates and photos to keep you informed and reassured.
  • Pick-up: When you return, you can pick up your dog, knowing that they've been well cared for and had a great time during their stay with us.
